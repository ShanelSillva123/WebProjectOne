Job Description

Marketing Manager

We are seeking an innovative marketing manager to promote our company's brand and services. You will be responsible for managing our marketing department's budget and staff, as well as preparing forecasts. An outstanding marketing manager will be someone whose expertise translates into increased brand awareness and profitability.

Working hours-flexiable-20 hours week normally 4 hours per day

A marketing manager is Resposibilities:

  1. Evaluating and optimizing marketing and pricing strategies.
  2. Analyzing market trends and preparing forecasts.
  3. Generating new business leads.
  4. Increasing brand awareness and market share.
  5. Coordinating marketing strategies with the sales, financial, public relations, and production departments.
  6. Developing and managing the marketing department's budget.
  7. Overseeing branding, advertising, and promotional campaigns.
  8. Managing the marketing department's staff.
  9. Preparing and presenting quarterly and annual reports to senior management.
  10. Promoting our brand at trade shows and major industry-related events.
  11. Keeping informed of marketing strategies and trends.

Qualifications

Essential

  • At least two years experience as a marketing manager.
  • Proficiency in electronic marketing automation software such as HubSpot Marketing and Bitrix24.
  • Extensive knowledge of marketing strategies, channels, and branding.
  • Bachelor's degree in marketing, finance, business administration, or similar.

Preferable

  1. A master's degree in a relevant field will be advantageous.
  2. Superb leadership, communication, and collaboration abilities.
  3. Exceptional analytical and problem-solving skills.
  4. Friendly and positive attitude
  5. Strong time management and organizational abilities

Key Resposibilities

  • Employee orientation and training logistics and recordkeeping
  • Company wide committee facilitation and participation
  • Employee safety, welfare, wellness and health reporting
  • Provide direct support to employees during implementation or HR services, policies, and programs

HR executive

HR executive provides consultation, steering and leadership to the broader HR team in building, documenting and optimizing HR business processes utilizing knowledge of HR processes, policies and leading practices.

An HR executive is expected:

Key Resposibilities

  • Ensure legal compliance.
  • Develops and maintains affirmative action program
  • Helps maintain human resource information system records and compiles reports
  • Maintain and update HRIS system to ensure accuracy of data – new hires, changes, exits
  • Manage work permit application, renewal and cancellation process
  • Prepare employment, salary increment, promotion letters
  • Maintain employee personal files – both hard copies and online copies
  • Coordinate logistics for new hires and exits

Qualifications

Essential

  1. Diploma or Bachelor's degree in Human Resource Management, Business or equivalent required
  2. Minimum 1 year of HR work experience for Executive application, ideally gained from Shared Service Center environment with SAP HR experience
  3. Excellent organizational skill, attention to details and is tenacious with follow-ups
  4. Experience with office space management

Preferable

  1. To focus on enhancing the value of human capital through development and HR initiatives Proficient in Microsoft Office SharePoint and Visio
  2. Highly motivated with a passion for performance-oriented work processes
  3. Must be extremely organized, detail-oriented and a collaborative team player
